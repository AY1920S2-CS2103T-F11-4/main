Cooking Papa

  • Cooking Papa is a desktop application that makes cooking at home easier. Cooking Papa provides a cookbook for users to search and add recipes. It also allows users to manage their ingredient inventory and shopping list with ease.

  • Target users are those who are interested to cook at home but finds it hard to manage their ingredients inventory and shopping list.
